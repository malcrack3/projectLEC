Good Luck, Babe!

by Chappell Roan

"Good Luck, Babe!" is a pop anthem by Chappell Roan that explores themes of desire, self-denial, and emotional vulnerability in modern relationships. With its catchy melody and introspective lyrics, the song captures the complexities of unspoken love.
